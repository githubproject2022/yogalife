Yo Gah!

Yoga workshops are not all the same! Chez Yo! Guys, no incense, Asian music and little lilac rugs.

Developed by men and for men, our workshops aim to achieve the goals that most men pursue: stay in shape, lose weight, strengthen or develop muscle mass, increase cardio-respiratory capacity.

We allow all of this while providing a fun activity surrounded by nice and friendly participants.

Musculation

Contrary to popular belief, yoga helps build muscle as well as weight training.

Cardio

Mental
